Please Ring the "Fish Doorbell"

Utrecht, the Netherlands’ fourth-largest city, is brimming with dams, locks, and canals. This means that the fish who migrate through the Dutch city are often stymied by these barriers. That’s where the “Fish Doorbell” comes in—a project enlisting the public’s help to ring the doorbell when fish are waiting, opening the lock and sending the fish through.

The Old Post Office Tower

Take in breathtaking views of Washington, D.C. from the Old Post Office Tower, the National Park Service's best-kept secret. From its observation deck, visitors can see the Washington Monument, the Capitol building, National Mall, and the Potomac River. Inside the tower, you can see the ropes that ring the Bells of Congress, the interior workings of the clock, and an exhibit about the Old Post Office Building’s history. Best of all, tours of the tower's observation deck are free year-round and do not require reservations.
